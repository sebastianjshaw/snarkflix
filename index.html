About Snarkflix

At Snarkflix, we are a small but mighty team of one reviewer, one Robot programed with nothing but snark and sarcasm and our goofy Bernedoodle sidekick. Despite our size, we are passionate about movies and love nothing more than sharing our thoughts and opinions with others. Our unique sense of humor sets us apart from the rest, as we take a lighthearted and entertaining approach to critiquing films.

We know that not every movie is a masterpiece, and even the best movies have their flaws. However, we believe that every film has something to offer, whether it's good, bad, or just plain weird. Our goal is not to tear down movies or insult filmmakers, but to provide our readers with an enjoyable and humorous perspective on cinema.

With over 1,495 movies (119 days of runtime) and 25,321 episodes across 738 days of television logged on Trakt, we've earned our credentials through sheer dedication to the craft of watching things. Every cinema visit, streaming binge, and questionable direct-to-video decision has been meticulously tracked, proving we really do have nothing better to do than judge your favorite films.

From 594 action movies to 172 horror films, 178 superhero flicks to 55 musicals, we've suffered through (sorry, carefully analyzed) nearly every genre imaginable. Whether it's blockbuster sci-fi (462 films), prestige drama (392), or the one deeply questionable Donghua entry in our database, we've seen it all. Our viewing history reads like a person who can't commit to a favorite genre and we're perfectly fine with that.

With our sharp wit, an ever-growing watchlist, and our adorable Bernedoodle companion, we're always ready to take on whatever movie comes our way. So if you're looking for a fresh take on the latest blockbuster or just want to share in our love of movies, join us at Snarkflix for a fun and snarky ride through the world of cinema!
